Training on the full dataset (~284 K rows) typically takes 3–8 minutes depending on hardware.
What happens
  • Dataset is loaded and validated from data/raw/creditcard.csv.
  • Amount and Time are standardised with StandardScaler.
  • 80/20 stratified train/test split is applied.
  • SMOTE is applied only on training data to prevent leakage.
  • All four models are trained and saved to models/.
  • Metrics (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1, ROC-AUC) are computed and stored.
